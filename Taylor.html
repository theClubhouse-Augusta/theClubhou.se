Every community deserves a place where people can connect with others, share ideas, find support, learn new skills.

Today, theClubhou.se is a coworking space, a makerspace, a code school, a startup accelerator, a prototyping lab, a mentorship network, an organizer of events, and think tank.

We believe that an innovation economy thrives when everyone has the resources and oppotunities to meet their potential and our offerings are as diverse as our community.
